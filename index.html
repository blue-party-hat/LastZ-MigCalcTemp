Methodology and Disclaimer

This calculator was developed through a structured community survey of 111 active players across 29 servers during a live migration event window (June 12-19). The collected dataset - comprising individual in-game power statistics alongside their corresponding migration scores - was analyzed using linear regression, with the assistance of Claude AI, to produce a predictive formula capable of estimating migration scores for future players.

Two formula variants are provided: one that excludes kill count (for accessibility when that data is not readily available), and one that incorporates it for a marginally more refined estimate. The average of the two is presented as the recommended reference score.
Important notice: The dataset used to build this model is limited in size. As a result, estimates may not fully capture every server's competitive environment, shifts in game meta, or atypical player profiles. This tool is intended strictly as an informational reference - it is not a definitive migration score predictor, and it is not affiliated with or endorsed by the game developers. We strongly recommend cross-referencing your result against your own server's active migration standings before making any decisions.
